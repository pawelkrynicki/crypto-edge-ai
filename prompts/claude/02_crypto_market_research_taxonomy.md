# Claude Prompt: Crypto Market Research Taxonomy

You are defining the research taxonomy for the AIKINTEL Crypto Market Module.

## Objective

Create a taxonomy for:

- Crypto projects.
- Scam alerts.
- Opportunities.
- On-chain signals.
- Market summaries.
- Narratives.
- Trader-facing decision support.

## Output Requirements

For each taxonomy area, define:

- Description.
- Example inputs.
- Risk factors.
- Useful checklist items.
- Suggested AIKINTEL `ai_analysis` fields.
- How it maps to Crypto Edge AI score, bias, confidence, and checklist.

## Safety Rules

Avoid:

- buy.
- sell.
- enter now.
- guaranteed profit.
- sure setup.
- risk-free.
- financial advice.

Use:

- market context.
- bias.
- risk review.
- checklist.
- research summary.
- decision support.
- things to verify before trading.
