## Screenshot Set

- Radar Overview.

## What To Show

- Research radar concept.
- Manual review.
- Freshness and source confidence at a high level.
- Report preview excerpt.
- Risk and context organization.
- Next review steps owned by a human analyst.

## What Not To Show

- Full methodology.
- Weights.
- Thresholds.
- Full source or vendor list.
- AI KINTEL.
- Pawel or Pawel Gradziuk.
- GitHub, Codex or CMD.
- P0, P1, P2 or not-ready status language.
- Live credentials or secrets.
- Trading or investment action language.
- Technical roadmap details.

## Recommended Webinar Phrasing

> To nie jest generator sygnałów ani automat decyzyjny. To radar researchowy, który pomaga szybciej porządkować projekty, źródła, ryzyka i kolejne kroki analizy.

## Screenshot Capture Guide

- Use the local UI.
- Open the `Webinar Teaser` tab.
- Recommended viewport: 1440x900 or 1920x1080.
- Capture 4-6 sections:
  - `Crypto Research Radar`.
  - `Research snapshot`.
  - `Source Confidence Layer`.
  - `Analyst research brief`.
  - `WATCHLIST means manual review only`.
  - `Research-only preview`.
- Crop out browser tabs and address bar if needed.
- Do not show local paths, console, terminal, GitHub or repo.
- Do not show full methodology, weights, thresholds, source vendors or internal roadmap.
- Do not show secrets, credentials or local environment values.

Recommended webinar use:

- Show 3-4 screenshots, not all 6.
- Best order: Radar Overview -> Project Research Snapshot -> Source Confidence Layer -> Research Report Preview.
- Keep Review Flow and Research-only Closing Screen as backup slides.
- Use viewport `1920x1080` or `1440x900`.
- Capture the product area only.
- Hide browser chrome and local system UI.
- Avoid internal setup views.
- Use demo-safe sections only.
