# Crypto Edge AI Market Intelligence Scope

## Scope Correction

Crypto Edge AI is the main module direction.

The market intelligence layer described in earlier documents is now understood as the data backing for Crypto Edge AI, not as a separate product called Crypto Market.

## Module Definition

Crypto Edge AI is a trading intelligence module for crypto traders. It combines:

- Project/token context.
- Opportunities and narratives.
- Scam/risk alerts.
- Market summary context.
- Sentiment and bias.
- Confidence scoring.
- Setup review.
- Pre-trade checklist.

## Market Intelligence Backing

The backing data layer may include:

- `crypto_projects`.
- `crypto_scam_alerts`.
- `crypto_opportunities`.
- `crypto_market_summaries`.
- Existing AIKINTEL Market News / Crypto when available.
- `crypto_onchain_metrics` later if useful.

## Existing AIKINTEL News

AIKINTEL already has Market News with a Crypto category, sentiment filters, and AI Analysis.

Crypto Edge AI should not duplicate that general news module. It should:

- Link to it.
- Reuse it as source context.
- Map relevant crypto news into setup review context.
- Summarize it where access is available.

## Must-Have Camp v1 Data Areas

- Project/token research list.
- Scam/risk alerts.
- Opportunities/narratives.
- Market summaries.
- Setup review mock.

## Later Data Areas

- On-chain metrics.
- User watchlist.
- User insights.
- Persisted setup reviews.

## Safety Boundary

The module must remain advisory and research-focused. It must not produce buy/sell instructions, automated trading, copy trading, or financial advice.
