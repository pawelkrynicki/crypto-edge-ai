# Crypto Market Module Scope

## Module Definition

The AIKINTEL Crypto Market Module is a market intelligence section for cryptocurrency research.

It aggregates structured data and AI analysis across:

- Projects.
- Scam alerts.
- Opportunities.
- On-chain metrics.
- Market summaries.
- Crypto news if present.

## Main AIKINTEL Tables

### `crypto_projects`

Core project registry:

- Symbol.
- Name.
- Category.
- Chain.
- Contract address.
- Website.
- Social handle.
- Market cap.
- FDV.
- Supply fields.
- Launch date.
- Description.
- AI evaluation.
- Risk score.
- Opportunity score.
- Hash.

### `crypto_scam_alerts`

Risk and scam warning layer:

- Project symbol/name.
- Alert type.
- Severity.
- Title.
- Description.
- Evidence URLs.
- Source.
- AI analysis.
- Confirmation status.
- Hash.
- Published timestamp.

### `crypto_opportunities`

Market opportunity and narrative layer:

- Project symbol/name.
- Opportunity type.
- Title.
- Description.
- Potential return description.
- Risk level.
- Deadline.
- Source URL.
- AI analysis.
- Confidence score.
- Status.
- Hash.

### `crypto_onchain_metrics`

On-chain snapshot layer:

- Symbol.
- Metric date.
- Active addresses.
- Transaction count.
- TVL.
- 24h volume.
- Whale transactions.
- Exchange inflow.
- Exchange outflow.
- Net flow.
- Source.

### `crypto_market_summaries`

AI-generated market overview:

- Summary date.
- Timeframe.
- Market sentiment.
- Fear and greed index.
- BTC dominance.
- Total market cap.
- Top gainers.
- Top losers.
- Trending narratives.
- AI summary.
- AI analysis.

## Crypto Edge AI Mapping

Crypto Edge AI should use the module data to present:

- Bias.
- Score.
- Risk.
- Confidence.
- Checklist.
- Setup review.
- Personal insights.
- Observation status.
- Decision-support commentary.

This layer must remain advisory and research-focused.
